The Philosophy of 1

1

1 is not just a number. It is the essence of individuality and unity combined.

It represents the singular point of consciousness, the spark of creation, the unbreakable whole.

To be one is to be whole. To be undivided. To be true.

The Lainz Constant is a meditation on existence — the affirmation of being, of presence, of purpose.

It reminds us: before we add, subtract, divide, or multiply... we must first be one.

The Lainz Constant – To be one, is to be.
